About Carbon Compass

Your Strategic Guide to Navigating CBAM

Carbon Compass is an AI-powered platform designed to help businesses confidently navigate the complexities of the European Union’s Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM) — a key policy ensuring imported goods reflect their true carbon footprint.

The system automatically analyzes your shipping documents or manually entered data to determine whether your goods fall under CBAM regulation. This eliminates uncertainty and provides clarity in your compliance process.

How the CBAM Fee Is Calculated

Once a product is identified as CBAM-relevant, Carbon Compass estimates the potential CBAM fee based on:

  • The product category and its embedded emissions,
  • The quantity of goods imported, and
  • The current EU Allowance (EUA) price (€/tCO₂e).

From Calculation to Strategy

Beyond simple estimation, Carbon Compass empowers your business with AI-driven recommendations to help reduce CBAM-related costs. These actionable strategies may include:

  • Sourcing from lower-emission producers,
  • Verifying supplier carbon data for accuracy,
  • Exploring alternative materials or cleaner logistics routes.

By combining regulatory intelligence with sustainability insights, Carbon Compass turns compliance challenges into opportunities for greener, more cost-efficient trade.
